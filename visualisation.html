Data Visualisation

Do you want to see your
data differently?



Does your company generate a lot of data? Do you visualise it on a spreadsheet? Do you use bar charts, pie charts, power point presentations? If you do then you need to be aware that there are other ways you could be visualising your data that offer signficant benefits - more clarity, more interaction and more data.


Not convinced? Then check out all the advantages of what we can do for you below.





Use Another dimension

01

Space

There is more space to display all the data. This could allow for better comparison between datasets or more interactive elements to help see the data.

More
02

Dimensions

Data with more dimensions can be shown more easily. Data that varies over time, for example, is ideally suited to visualising in three dimensions.

More
03

Viewing

The data can be viewed from any angle and manipulated to make viewing easier.

More




Utilise the Technology

04

Structures

What better way to show structures that are naturally three-dimensional, such as medical imaging or architecture.

More
05

Interaction

A combination of web and graphics technology allows for highly interactive applications. We can filter data - hide, scale, emphasise or show any emerging trends.

More
06

Sharing

The web is the perfect platfomr to share all your data and view it on a large number of different devices and operating systems.

More

Empower your visualisations using three dimensions

If you are visualising your data using traditional methods such as barcharts, dashboards or excel spreadsheets then consider the benefits of turning to web based three dimensional visualisations.
